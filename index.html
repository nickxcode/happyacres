About us

Your Family's Favorite Outdoor Getaway

Happy Acres Campground was born from a simple dream: to create a place where families could escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and reconnect with nature. Nestled in the heart of Bristol, WI. Our campground has become a beloved destination for outdoor enthusiasts, adventure seekers, and families looking for fun and relaxation in the great outdoors.

Nearby Attractions

Summerfest - Duration 60min

Summerfest, held annually in downtown Milwaukee, Wisconsin, is renowned as "The World's Largest Music Festival." This multi-day event, first held in 1968, features hundreds of performances across various genres on 12 stages. Located at Henry Maier Festival Park along Lake Michigan, it attracts around 800,000 visitors each year. Beyond music, Summerfest offers food, shopping, and activities for all ages, including a dedicated children’s area, making it a diverse and family-friendly festival.

Six Flags Great America - Duration 40min

Six Flags Great America, located in Gurnee, Illinois, is a 304-acre amusement park known for its thrilling roller coasters, with 15 in total, ranking it among the world's leaders. The park features twelve themed areas, including a 20-acre water park, Hurricane Harbor Chicago.

Milwaukee Tour - Duration 60min

In Milwaukee, visitors can explore the vibrant arts scene, visit the famous Harley-Davidson Museum, and enjoy tours of local breweries. The city's historical architecture, including the Basilica of St. Josaphat and Pabst Mansion, offers a glimpse into its rich heritage. The Milwaukee Riverwalk provides a scenic route to explore downtown, and the Milwaukee Public Market is a must-visit for food enthusiasts. Additionally, the Milwaukee Art Museum and Discovery World offer engaging experiences for culture and science lovers.

Chicago Tour - Duration 120min

In Chicago, embark on an architectural river cruise, visit iconic landmarks like the Willis Tower and Navy Pier, explore world-class museums like the Art Institute, and enjoy the bustling Millennium Park. The city's vibrant food scene, encompassing deep-dish pizza and diverse culinary delights, is a must-try. For shopping and entertainment, the Magnificent Mile is a top destination.
